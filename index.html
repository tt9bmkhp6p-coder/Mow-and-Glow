— 02 / How it works

Booking is
boringly simple.

No app to download, no annual contract, no upsell on the doorstep. Tell us the address, we send a quote, and we get on the schedule.

STEP 01

Tell us about your place

Fill out the form, shoot us a text, or call. Photos of the yard or car help but aren't required.

STEP 02

Flat-rate quote in 24h

You get a written quote with one clear price — weekly, monthly, or one-time. No fine print.

STEP 03

Pick a day that works

We give you a recurring slot and stick to it. Reschedules are easy — just text us and we'll move it.

STEP 04

We show up, you don't move

You get a "we're here" text and an "all done" text with a photo when we finish. That's it.
